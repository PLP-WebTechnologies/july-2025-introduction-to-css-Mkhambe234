Chocolate Chip Cookies

Delicious and easy-to-make chocolate chip cookies!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the flour and baking soda.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  4. Mix in the egg and vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto an ungreased baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown.
  9. Allow to cool before serving. Enjoy!
